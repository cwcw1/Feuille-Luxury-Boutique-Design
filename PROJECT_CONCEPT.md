## ðŸ’¡ Design Philosophy

### **"Elegant Minimalism with Purposeful Animation"**

**Core Principles:**

1. **Restraint = Luxury**
   - Every animation serves a purpose
   - No unnecessary decoration
   - Subtle, refined movements

2. **Slow & Deliberate**
   - 0.8s animations (not rushed)
   - Smooth easing (natural deceleration)
   - Time to appreciate details

3. **Progressive Disclosure**
   - Content reveals as you scroll
   - Creates discovery and anticipation
   - Prevents overwhelm

4. **Gallery Presentation**
   - Staggered reveals like a personal stylist
   - Each product gets its moment
   - Curated, not catalog
